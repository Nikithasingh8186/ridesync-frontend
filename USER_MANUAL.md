# RideSync — User Manual

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
- Open RideSync at http://localhost:5173
- Click Register
- Enter your name, work email, employee ID
- Select your home zone (the area you live in)
- Set a password and click Create account

### 2. Sign In
- Enter your work email and password
- Click Sign in
- You will land on the Find a Ride screen

---

## Finding a Ride

### Browse Available Rides
- The Find a Ride tab shows all open rides
- Rides are sorted by match score (0-100)
  - 100 = same zone + exact time
  - 50 = same zone, different time
  - 25 = nearby zone

### Join a Ride
- Click Join ride on any available ride
- The seat count updates immediately
- The driver will see your name on their ride

### What the Match Score Means

Score    Meaning
80-100   Perfect match — same zone, close time
50-79    Good match — same zone
25-49    Nearby zone
0-24     Not shown

---

## Offering a Ride

### Post Your Ride
1. Click the Offer a ride tab
2. Select your pickup zone
3. Enter your pickup landmark
4. Set date and time
5. Choose how many seats you have
6. Add your vehicle details (optional but helpful)
7. Tick Repeat every weekday if this is your daily commute
8. Click Post ride

### Managing Your Ride
- Go to My Rides to see who has joined
- You can cancel a ride before departure
- Riders are notified when you cancel

---

## My Rides

### As a Passenger
- See all rides you have joined under Rides you have joined
- Click Leave ride to cancel your booking

### As a Driver
- See all rides you have posted under Rides you are driving
- See how many seats are filled
- Cancel if your plans change

---

## Stats and Savings

### What is Tracked
- Rs Saved — estimated savings vs booking a solo cab
- CO2 Avoided — kg of CO2 not emitted by sharing
- Rides Shared — total number of shared trips

### Calculation Method
- Solo cab assumed at Rs 300 per trip (Hyderabad average)
- Shared cost = Rs 300 divided by number of riders
- CO2 saved = 120g/km x route distance x (riders - 1)

## AI Suggestions
- RideSync shows a personalized suggestion on your home screen
- It analyzes your zone, usual time, and past ride partners
- Example: Priya usually drives at 9 AM from Banjara Hills —
  you have saved Rs 540 riding together this month.

---

## Tips
- Set your home zone accurately — it is the number 1 factor in matching
- Post recurring rides to build a reliable carpool group
- The earlier you post, the more riders can join
- Coordinate exact pickup spot via your company chat
